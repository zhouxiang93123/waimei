<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1958</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，河南省修武县人，基督徒，维权公民，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代末，曾因家中惨遭两次政府不当拆迁，损失巨大，其被迫提起诉讼，打赢了官司，但因被告是当地政府，法院不予执行，故而其被迫上访维权；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李玉凤曾因不断维权，被当局进行恶意打击报复，并以“妨害公务罪”对其判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；出狱后其继续状告地方政府，再遭逼迫，又被劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；两次入狱，备受酷刑折磨。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日因与他人前往监狱迎接“新公民运动”维权代表袁冬出狱，并率先打出横幅“欢迎袁冬从小监狱回到大监狱”，又被北京大兴区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日被北京市大兴区检察院批捕，至<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日被取保候审释放；关押期间，曾被突击审讯（且大多是疲劳审讯）达<span lang="EN-US">50</span>余次，身心惨遭迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，又因在北京南站照相被北京市丰台公安刑拘一个月后，交由焦作市当局带回原籍，补充侦查再次被刑拘，后转正式逮捕，被关押在河南省焦作看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被河南省焦作市中站区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，并在郑州女子监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因其与同伴封西霞到北京最高法院申诉，又到河南省法院驻京办事处反映司法腐败制造冤假错案问题，随遭焦作维稳办人员抓捕、刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
