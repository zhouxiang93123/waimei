<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>&nbsp;<p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：新疆察布查尔县土地承包人亲属再次向自治区高院上诉，再次提起上诉的是新疆察布查尔锡伯自治县（以下简称察县）的土地承包人沈志和的妻子李艳茹、沈志和的母亲郜世英及沈志和的子女等三代六人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">沈志和是新疆伊犁哈萨克自治州察布查尔锡伯自治县城镇蒙霍尔村的农民。<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年，他响应县政府发展经济、鼓励县内外人士投资，承包、开发土地的号召，和几个亲戚贷款筹资，分别与县良种繁育场和蒙霍尔村签订《协议》，承包开发县良种繁育场土地<span lang="EN-US">1200</span>亩，承包开发蒙霍尔村土地<span lang="EN-US">1200</span>亩，承包期为<span lang="EN-US">30</span>年，承包费为每亩每年<span lang="EN-US">30</span>元。经县政府核查，<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，沈志和与县国土局重新签订土地承包《合同》，原土地承包《协议》入档。<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年、<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年，县政府又两次重新核查了土地，确认了沈志和承包的土地。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为开发承包的土地，沈志和与几个合伙人共投资了<span lang="EN-US">1700</span>多万元，平整土地、捡地里的石头、挖排碱渠、打了<span lang="EN-US">6</span>口灌溉机井，配有变压器、潜水泵，修了<span lang="EN-US">3</span>公里的道路，架设<span lang="EN-US">6</span>公里多的高压输电线路，种树<span lang="EN-US">4900</span>多棵，建牛羊圈舍<span lang="EN-US">1450</span>平方米，砖木结构“三油两毡”的房舍<span lang="EN-US">3250</span>平方米，购买大型农机具花了<span lang="EN-US">80</span>多万元。他们常年雇请维吾尔、哈萨克、回、锡伯、蒙等少数民族农民劳作耕种，每年仅付工资一项就达<span lang="EN-US">120</span>多万。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">没想到察布查尔县鼓励人们承包开发土地的政策突然变了。<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年，县上空降来个王奕文书记，搞 “土地清查”，说包括沈志和在内的三四百户承包土地“违法”，全部没收，并且对我们的投资不给补偿。<span lang="EN-US">,<o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，察布查尔县国土局向沈志和送达《解除合同通知书》，没收沈志和承包的所有土地，不准沈志和及合伙人、雇佣的农民进地耕种。到<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年，这<span lang="EN-US">2400</span>亩地全部被政府租赁给其他人耕种，租赁费涨到每年每亩<span lang="EN-US">300</span>元（现在租地费用涨到了每亩<span lang="EN-US">600</span>元到<span lang="EN-US">900</span>元不等）。沈志和承受不了巨大的损失，拒绝在政府的毁约书上签字，到察县政府信访局、伊犁哈萨克自治州政府信访局、新疆维吾尔自治区政府信访局、北京国务院信访局上访。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">由于上访，沈志和于<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日被察县公安局刑事拘留，后经察县检察院公诉，察县法院于<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日作出一审判决：认定沈志和犯非法占用农用地罪，判处有期徒刑二年，缓刑三年。<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，伊犁州中院作出行政裁定，认定沈志和犯非法占用农用地罪，驳回上诉，维持原判。沈志和不服县、州两级法院的判决，继续签字与土地遭没收的其它承包人到伊犁州、新疆维吾尔自治区政府上访。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，察县法院决定对沈志和取消<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年缓刑，执行<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年有期徒刑的实刑，沈志和被押送到新源监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，沈志和因病获得监外执行，在监狱服刑近八个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">沈志和刑满后于<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日向新疆维吾尔自治区高级人民法院伊犁哈萨克自治州分院提起行政诉讼，诉求察县政府依法继续履行两个面积各为<span lang="EN-US">1200</span>亩的国有土地使用权租赁合同，并要求察县政府赔偿因非法没收土地从<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年至<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年间给沈志和造成的直接经济损失<span lang="EN-US">923</span>万元。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，新疆高院伊犁州分院做出一审判决，判处察县政府和察县自然资源局（原察县国土局）赔偿沈志和承包地上附着物（被告非法单方评估）<span lang="EN-US">200.53</span>万元，驳回沈志和的其它诉求。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">沈志和不服一审判决，上诉到新疆维吾尔自治区高级人民法院。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">沈志和于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日因病去世。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">新疆高院<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>号作出行政裁定，撤销一审判决发回伊犁州分院重审。伊犁州分院在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日二审作出判决，两个月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>天后，沈志和的第一顺序继承人李艳茹等六人才于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日取得伊犁州分院重审《行政判决书》。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
